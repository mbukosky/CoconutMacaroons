LAUREN MELEY

ACTRESS and WRITER

Thanks for checking out my site! I am an actress and writer based in Baltimore, MD. I lived and trained in New York City for seven years, where my credits included the Off-Broadway production of The Columbine Project and the AEA Showcase of Arthur Miller's All My Sons at The Morningside Players. My film credits consist of a wide-range of indie projects, including the award-winning shorts BY THE WAKE and CAROLINE OF VIRGINIA. I've also written a handful of short films, including the quirky romantic comedy BEAR NECESSITY which was produced in New York. I grew up in Baltimore as a dancer, and developed a strong sense of discipline and primal storytelling that I carry with me everyday as an actor. You can check out my full resume, photos and videos on this site, and more on IMDb, Instagram , Twitter.
