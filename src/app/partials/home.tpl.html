BIO

Thanks for checking out my site! Here's a little bit about me. My theatrical credits include the Off-Broadway production of The Columbine Project and, most recently, playing the role of Ann Deever in All My Sons at The Morningside Players. My film credits include a wide-range of indie projects, including the award-winning short CAROLINE OF VIRGINIA and the upcoming sci-fi feature THE SPACESHIP (2015). I've studied at T. Schreiber Studio and Penny Templeton Studio. I continue to train and coach with Penny, and both studios continue to offer a creative home where I will be forever grateful. In between acting jobs, I run scavenger hunts throughout the city (yes, it's as fun as it sounds!) and dabble in writing. I've written a few short films; one quirky romantic comedy called BEAR NECESSITY was produced and is currently making the festival circuit. I grew up in Baltimore as a dancer, and developed a strong sense of discipline and primal storytelling that I carry with me everyday as an actor. I continue to take dance classes around the city, at Joffrey Ballet School and 92nd Street Y. You can check out my full resume, photos and videos on this site, and more on IMDb, Facebook, Twitter.
