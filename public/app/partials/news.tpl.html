The Green Sweater

8/12/16

I'm starring in a staged reading this weekend for a wonderfully moving play, The Green Sweater. Readings are Sat, August 13 @ 8pm and Sun, August 14 @ 2pm at United Evangelical Church (home of Cohesion Theatre Company) 3900 Dillon St., Baltimore, MD 21224.

About The Green Sweater: Parker is a young woman in her mid-twenties who is struggling to get her life together. Her deep and sometimes crippling depression has caused her to fall into a pattern of loneliness and lack of motivation. Though surrounded by close friends, she often feels alone. Currently working as a waitress, she doesn’t seem to have any career goals. And having recently been rejected by her friend, Blake, she has no romantic aspirations either. The Green Sweater follows Parker as she navigates her friendships, a new relationship, and her depression through life, love, and tragedy. Co-Directed by Hannah Fogler and Alexander Scally.

And it's free! Come join us!
