BIO

Thanks for checking out my site! Here's a little bit about me. My theatrical credits include the Off-Broadway production of The Columbine Project and, most recently, playing the role of Ann Deever in a production of All My Sons at The Morningside Players. My film credits include a wide-range of indie projects, including the award-winning short CAROLINE OF VIRGINIA and the upcoming sci-fi feature THE SPACESHIP (2015). I trained three years at T. Schreiber Studio (Six-week Conservatory, and Scene Study with Peter Jensen and Terry Schreiber) and two years at Penny Templeton Studio (Advanced On-Camera and Master Scene Study). I continue to train and coach with Penny, and both studios continue to offer a creative home where I will be forever grateful. In between acting jobs I run scavenger hunts throughout the city (yes, it's as fun as it sounds!) and dabble in writing. I've written a couple short films; one quirky romantic comedy called BEAR NECESSITY was produced and is currently making the festival circuit. But my heart and soul will always be onstage or in front of the camera; it's a comforting, magical place. I grew up in Baltimore as a dancer, and developed a strong sense of discipline and primal storytelling that I carry with me everyday as an actor. I continue to take dance classes around the city, at Joffrey Ballet School and 92nd Street Y. You can check out my full resume, photos and videos on this site, and more on IMDb, Facebook, Twitter.
